The Major's suite
, an epitome of timeless elegance, welcomes guests into a realm of classic luxury and comfort. The room features deep burgundy and soft gold tones that create a sophisticated atmosphere, with walls adorned in intricate damask wallpaper. At the center is a grand four-poster bed draped in sumptuous linens, flanked by antique nightstands with ornate lamps casting a warm, inviting glow. High ceilings and tall windows, dressed in flowing drapes, enhance the sense of space and openness while allowing natural light to highlight the room’s exquisite details.

A plush sitting area
, thoughtfully arranged near the window, includes elegant wingback chairs and a vintage writing desk. This cozy nook is perfect for reading or relaxing, offering a lovely view of the surrounding gardens. A stately fireplace with an intricately carved mantel adds charm and warmth, creating a comforting ambiance for guests to unwind.

The en-suite bathroom
, a luxurious retreat, features a deep clawfoot tub against marble tiles and polished brass fixtures. The spacious shower area, equipped with a rainfall showerhead, promises a rejuvenating experience. Fluffy bathrobes and plush towels add to the indulgence, making the Major’s Suite not just a room but a personal sanctuary designed for an unforgettable stay.
