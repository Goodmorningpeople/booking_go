The General's quarter
exudes a commanding presence with its regal decor and sophisticated ambiance, meticulously crafted to offer an unforgettable stay. As you enter, you're immediately greeted by a spacious layout adorned with rich, dark wood furnishings and luxurious, velvet drapery that frames tall windows. The centerpiece of the room is an opulent four-poster bed draped in high-thread-count linens, inviting you to sink into its plush embrace. Antique military memorabilia and framed portraits of historic figures add a distinguished touch, reflecting the suite's namesake and evoking a sense of grandeur from a bygone era.

The quarter's
separate sitting area is equally impressive, featuring a cozy, leather tufted sofa and a pair of elegant armchairs positioned around a stately fireplace. The warm glow of the fire, complemented by the soft, ambient lighting of vintage sconces, creates an intimate atmosphere perfect for unwinding with a book or enjoying a quiet conversation. A polished mahogany desk stands ready for any correspondence or planning you might undertake, while a selection of fine, classic literature and maps of historical battles offer a nod to the suite’s storied inspiration.

The en-suite bathroom
continues the theme of luxury and comfort, boasting a deep clawfoot tub ideal for a relaxing soak after a day of exploration. Marble countertops and an antique brass shower fixture complete the look, ensuring every detail contributes to a sense of refinement. Plush bathrobes and high-end toiletries are provided to enhance your pampering experience, making the General's Suite not just a room, but a haven of elegance and historical charm that promises a memorable retreat.
